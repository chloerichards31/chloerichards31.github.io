EO Community Catalyst

Co-founding a community from scratch

I believe great communities don't just happen, they're built with intention. As an elected Employee-Ownership Trustee at I-COM since October 2024, I channelled that belief into the EO Community Catalyst. From concept to delivery, I've been at the heart of every event, shaping the agenda, producing the content, and working to create something that truly serves the North West EO community. The series is a partnership between the team at I-COM and the team at Still We Grow, brought to life through genuine collaboration between both organisations.

70+

The North West's first community-led EO event series

Co-founded and delivered in partnership between the teams at I-COM and Still We Grow, the inaugural event in November 2025 brought together over 40 employee-owners, trustees and independent trustees at Campfield, Manchester. Since then the community has grown to 70+ members, with a second event in March 2026 and more to come. Built by the community, for the community.
